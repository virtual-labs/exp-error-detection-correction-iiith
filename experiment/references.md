### Textbooks

**1. Error Control Coding**<br/>
BY: S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of error detection and correction, linear block codes, standard array decoding, syndrome decoding, and maximum likelihood decoding._

**2. The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: F. J. MacWilliams and N. J. A. Sloane - North-Holland, 1977<br/>
_Classic reference on algebraic coding theory covering standard array construction, syndrome computation, and decoding algorithms._

**3. Fundamentals of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: W. Huffman and V. Pless - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Detailed treatment of linear codes, coset decomposition, standard array, and syndrome-based decoding techniques._

**4. A First Course in Coding Theory**<br/>
BY: Raymond Hill - Oxford University Press, 1986<br/>
_Accessible introduction to error detection and correction with clear explanations of standard array and syndrome decoding._

**5. Introduction to Coding Theory**<br/>
BY: J. H. van Lint - Springer, 1999<br/>
_Mathematical foundations of coding theory including error detection, correction capabilities, and decoding methods._

**6. Introduction to the Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: Vera Pless - Wiley-Interscience, 1982<br/>
_Introduction to algebraic coding theory with focus on linear codes, BCH codes, and decoding algorithms including syndrome-based methods._
